Venture idea due dilligence

Ultimately, after a two-week exploration, we decided the idea was not attractive-enough to pursue further exploration.

In order to assess the merit of the idea, I first crafted and evaluation framework : (i) how big is the market?, (ii) how meaningful is the idea?, (iii) is there an unfair advantage with which to get started, (iv) how large is the problem relative to alternatives?

To answer these questions, I conducted desktop research and lined up ~15 interviews with friends and folks available via reddit. The idea related apartment leasing, and thefore interviewed tenants, landlords, and real estate agents.
